FAQ

What is DesignGhar?

We are a platform between customers and designers from all around the world both customers and designers are given options and freedom to choose their deigner and customers respectively.

Is it good for me?

For Customer: Our customer gets to choose his plan no matter how small it is and get to choose between the designers that have applied on various statistics.
For Designer: Our designer gets to choose his projects according to his will from the last customers list provided and budget provided by customer.

Why DesignGhar?

It allows both designer and customers to choose freely among themselves with their own will without getting anyone involved in their decision.
